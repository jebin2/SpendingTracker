# AI Spending Analysis

Generates a breakdown of your spending for a chosen time period, with category totals, AI insights, and money-saving tips.

---

## How to use it

Open the **Insights** tab and tap **Generate Analysis**. Choose last 7 days, this month, or this year.

Analysis for all three periods also runs **automatically every day at 12:00 PM** as part of the scheduled jobs — so by the time you open the tab, fresh insights are usually already waiting.

---

## What happens step by step

1. After tapping the button, a **loading spinner** appears while the analysis is being prepared in the background.

2. You can navigate away and come back — the spinner persists across page refreshes because the status is tracked in the sheet, not just in memory.

3. When ready, the results appear:
   - Total amount spent for the period
   - Breakdown by category with percentage bars
   - AI-written insights about your spending patterns
   - Personalised saving tips with an estimated saving for each

4. If the analysis fails, a **Retry** button appears. The daily schedule also retries it automatically.

---

## Refreshing

Tap **Refresh** to force a new analysis. Results are reused for up to 24 hours by default so you do not have to wait each time you open the tab.

---

## Related features

- **Compare Prices** tab uses the same system to compare merchants side by side.
- **Scheduled Tasks** (Settings) shows the last run time for each period and lets you trigger analysis manually.
