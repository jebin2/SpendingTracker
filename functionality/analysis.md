# AI Spending Analysis

Generates a breakdown of your spending for a chosen time period, with category totals, AI insights, and money-saving tips.

---

## How to use it

Open the Insights tab in the app and tap **Generate Analysis**. You can choose to analyse the current week, month, or year.

---

## What happens step by step

1. After you tap the button, a **loading spinner** appears while the analysis is being prepared.

2. The analysis runs in the background — you can navigate away and come back. The spinner persists across page refreshes.

3. When the analysis is ready, the spinner disappears and the results appear:
   - Total amount spent for the period
   - A breakdown by category with percentage bars
   - AI-written insights about your spending patterns
   - Personalised tips with an estimated potential saving for each

4. If the analysis fails, a **Retry** button appears. The app also retries automatically overnight.

---

## Refreshing

Tap **Refresh** to force a new analysis. By default, results are reused for up to 24 hours so you do not have to wait each time you open the tab.

---

## Related features
