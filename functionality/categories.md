# Categories

Lets you view the built-in spending categories and add your own custom ones for organising transactions your way.

---

## How to use it

Go to **Settings**, then **AI & Analysis**, then tap **Categories**.

---

## What happens step by step

1. The Categories page opens, showing the default categories that come built into the app — such as Food & Dining, Transport, Shopping, and Health. These cannot be edited or deleted.

2. Below the defaults, any custom categories you have created are listed, each with a delete button.

3. To add a new category, tap **Add custom**. A small form appears where you choose an emoji icon and type a name.

4. Tap **Save**. The new category appears in the list immediately and is now available to assign to transactions.

5. To remove a custom category, tap the delete icon next to it. It disappears from the list right away.

---

## Default categories

The following categories are built in and available to all users: Food & Dining, Transport, Shopping, Entertainment, Health, Bills & Utilities, Education, Personal Care, Gifts & Donations, and Others.

---

## Note

Only custom categories can be deleted. Deleting a category does not affect any transactions already assigned to it.
