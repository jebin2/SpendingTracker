# Compare Prices

Lets you pick two to four merchants you have spent money at and get an AI-powered side-by-side comparison of how they stack up on price, value, and quality.

---

## How to use it

Open the **Compare** tab on the Insights screen. Search for merchants from your transaction history, select two to four, and tap **Compare with AI**.

---

## What happens step by step

1. You see a list of merchants drawn from your past transactions. Type to search and tap merchants to select them — selected names appear as chips at the top.

2. Once you have selected at least two, tap **Compare with AI**. A spinner appears while the comparison is being prepared.

3. The comparison runs in the background. You can navigate away; the spinner persists if you return to the tab.

4. When ready, the results appear:
   - A **verdict** summarising which merchant comes out on top
   - Score cards for each aspect (price, value, quality, etc.) with a winner highlighted for each
   - A **recommendation** at the bottom

5. If the comparison fails, a **Retry** button appears. The app also retries automatically overnight.

---

## Refreshing

Tap **Refresh** to run a fresh comparison. Results are reused for up to 24 hours by default.

---

## Related features

The Insights tab's spending analysis uses the same time periods (week, month, year) to filter the data used in comparisons.
