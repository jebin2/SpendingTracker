# Dashboard

The home screen — shows a spending summary for the selected period, a chart of spending by category, recent transactions, and reminders for recurring payments that are due.

---

## What you see

**Greeting and period selector** at the top — tap This Week, This Month, or This Year to switch the view.

**Spending chart** — a visual breakdown of how much was spent per category in the selected period. Gives a quick at-a-glance picture of where money is going.

**Total spent** — the sum of all transactions in the selected period shown prominently.

**Recurring payment reminders** — if you have set up recurring transactions (e.g. a monthly rent or subscription), a card appears when one is overdue or due today. Tap it to log it immediately with pre-filled details.

**Recent transactions** — the latest few transactions listed below the chart. Tap any row to open the full transaction detail.

---

## Navigating from the dashboard

- Tap any transaction row to view or edit its details.
- Tap **Add** (the + button) to log a new transaction manually.
- Tap **Capture** to log via SMS, photo, or paste.

---

## Data freshness

The dashboard loads instantly from locally cached data and quietly refreshes in the background. If you are offline, the last synced data is shown.
