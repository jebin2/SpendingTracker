# Profile & Location Settings

Found in **Settings → Profile & Location**. Personalises how AI analyses and categorises your spending.

---

## What you can set

**City / Region** — your location (e.g. Bangalore, Mumbai). AI uses this when parsing SMS and emails to understand local merchant names, currencies, and spending patterns.

**Lifestyle tags** — select tags that describe you: Vegetarian, Budget-conscious, Student, Frequent traveller, Family, Health-conscious, etc. AI uses these to give more relevant insights in the Analysis tab — for example, flagging if your food spending seems high for a vegetarian lifestyle.

**Monthly income** — optional. If set, the Analysis tab can show what percentage of income was spent in a period.

---

## How changes take effect

Settings are saved to your Google Sheet immediately when you tap **Save**. The next time an AI analysis or email import runs, it picks up the updated values automatically.

---

## Why these matter

Without a region set, AI may misidentify local merchants or use the wrong currency context. Without lifestyle tags, the analysis insights are more generic. These fields are entirely optional — the app works without them.
