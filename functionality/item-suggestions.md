# Item Suggestions

Automatically spots inconsistent item names and missing details across your transactions and offers clean-up suggestions you can accept or dismiss with one tap.

---

## How to use it

Suggestions appear on their own — no action needed to start the process. When AI finds something to fix, a small **AI chip** appears on the transaction in your list. Tap it to see the suggestions.

---

## What happens step by step

1. Each time you open the Transactions screen, a background check runs quietly to look for two kinds of issues:
   - **Spelling variations** — the same item recorded as "masala dosa", "Masala Dosa", and "msla dosa" across different transactions
   - **Missing details** — information in your notes that could be moved into the item name, quantity, or merchant fields

2. When issues are found, a small **AI chip** appears on the affected transaction card.

3. Tap the chip to open the **Suggestions panel**. Each suggestion shows the current value crossed out and the recommended replacement in bold.

4. Tap **Use** to apply the suggestion, or **No** to dismiss it.

5. If you accept a spelling fix, it is applied to every transaction with that same item name — not just the one you tapped.

---

## Cooldown

The background check runs at most once per hour to avoid slowing things down. If you open the app again within an hour, the check is skipped silently.

---

## Related features

The item name is also used in Spending Analysis category breakdowns, so cleaning up names helps make your analysis more accurate.
