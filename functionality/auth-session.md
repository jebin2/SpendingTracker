# Sign In and Session

Signs you in with your Google account and keeps you logged in automatically, even when your session token expires in the background.

---

## How to use it

On the FundsFlee home screen, tap **Sign in with Google**. You will be taken to Google's login page to grant access, then brought back into the app.

---

## What happens step by step

1. You tap **Sign in with Google** and are redirected to Google's login page.

2. Google asks you to approve access to your Gmail, Google Sheets, and Google Drive. Tap **Allow**.

3. You are brought back into FundsFlee. If this is your first time, a private Google Sheet is created in your Drive to store all your transactions. This takes just a moment.

4. First-time users are taken to a short **onboarding screen** to set your region and spending preferences.

5. From then on, you stay signed in automatically. If your Google login expires in the background, FundsFlee silently renews it without interrupting you.

6. If automatic renewal fails, you will be asked to sign in again — just tap **Sign in with Google** one more time.

---

## Signing out

Go to **Settings** and tap **Sign Out**. You will be returned to the home screen.

---

## Note on permissions

FundsFlee only accesses data it created. It reads Gmail to import bank emails, uses Google Sheets to store your transactions, and uses Google Drive to save receipt images.
