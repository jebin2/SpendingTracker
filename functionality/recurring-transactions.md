# Recurring Transactions

Reminds you to log expenses that happen on a regular schedule — such as a monthly subscription or a weekly grocery run — and lets you record them with one tap.

---

## How to use it

When adding a manual transaction, scroll to the **Recurrence** field and choose how often it repeats: daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. Save the transaction as normal.

---

## What happens step by step

1. Each time you open the Dashboard, the app checks whether any recurring transaction is due today or overdue.

2. If something is due, a **"Recurring due"** section appears above your recent transactions. Each card shows the merchant or item name, the last amount, and how long it has been since the last entry.

3. Cards that are overdue show a **red border** so they stand out.

4. Tap **Log it** on a card to record a new occurrence using the same details as last time. The card disappears from the list once logged.

5. The next due date is automatically calculated based on the recurrence you set, so the reminder comes back at the right time.

---

## Recurrence options

- **Daily** — due every day
- **Weekly** — due every 7 days
- **Monthly** — due on the same date each month
- **Yearly** — due on the same date each year

---

## Note

Recurring transactions are independent entries. If the amount changes, just edit the logged transaction — the next reminder will still use the latest logged amount as a reference.
