# Push Notifications

Sends you a notification on your device when a receipt has finished being read, so you know your transactions are ready without having to keep the app open.

---

## How to use it

Go to **Settings** and tap **Turn on** under "Receipt notifications". Your browser will ask for permission — tap **Allow**.

To stop receiving notifications, go back to Settings and tap **Turn off**.

---

## What happens step by step

1. After you turn on notifications, your browser registers for push alerts and saves the subscription to your account.

2. Whenever you photograph a receipt and the AI finishes reading it, a notification is sent to your device — even if the app is not open.

3. The notification shows the merchant name, how many items were found, and the total amount. For example: "Zomato receipt processed — 3 items · ₹346".

4. Tapping the notification opens the app directly to your **Transactions** list so you can review what was added.

---

## Availability

Push notifications require a browser that supports web notifications (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox on Android or desktop). They are not available in Safari on iOS.

---

## Related features

Notifications are sent after receipt photos are processed. See the **Receipt Camera** feature for how receipts are captured and read.
