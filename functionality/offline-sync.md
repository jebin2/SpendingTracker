# Offline Sync

Keeps the app fully usable when you have no internet connection, and automatically syncs your changes when you reconnect.

---

## How to use it

There is nothing to set up. FundsFlee works offline automatically. Just use the app as normal — adding, editing, or deleting transactions — and any changes made without a connection will be uploaded as soon as you are back online.

---

## What happens step by step

1. When your device goes offline, the app detects this and keeps working. You can still add transactions, view your history, and browse all your data.

2. Any changes you make while offline are saved to your device. A small **pending badge** appears to show how many items are queued for upload.

3. When your connection returns, the queued changes are uploaded automatically in the background — even if the app tab is closed.

4. Once syncing is complete, the badge disappears and your transaction list refreshes to show the latest data from the server.

5. If a change cannot be applied — for example, if you edited a transaction that was deleted from another device — it moves to a **conflicts** count. A badge lets you know something needs attention.

---

## What it looks like

The pending badge appears near the network indicator when you are offline or syncing. Once everything is up to date, it disappears.

---

## Note

Your recent transactions are always cached on your device, so the app opens instantly even before the network loads fresh data.
