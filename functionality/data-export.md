# Data & Export

Found in **Settings → Data & Export**. Options for getting your data out of the app or viewing it directly.

---

## Export as CSV

Tap **Export as CSV** to download all your transactions as a spreadsheet file. The file includes: date, time, merchant, category, subcategory, amount, payment method, source, notes, and tags.

The export uses whatever transactions are currently loaded — it works offline too since it reads from the local cache.

---

## Open in Google Sheets

Tap **Open in Google Sheets** to jump directly to your raw data in the browser. This opens the exact spreadsheet where all your transactions are stored, so you can apply your own filters, formulas, or charts.

---

## Danger Zone

**Refresh App** — clears all cached files and reloads the latest version of the app. Useful after an update if you want to make sure you have the newest version. See the Refresh App feature for details.

**Clear local cache** — removes locally saved preferences (region, lifestyle tags). Your data in Google Sheets is completely unaffected. The app returns to a fresh state on next load.
