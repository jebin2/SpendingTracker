# Refresh App

Clears the app's saved cache and downloads the latest version, useful if the app feels out of date or is behaving unexpectedly.

---

## How to use it

Go to **Settings**, scroll to the **Danger Zone** section, and tap **Refresh App**. A confirmation prompt will appear — confirm to proceed.

---

## What happens step by step

1. After you confirm, the button shows a spinner while the refresh is in progress.

2. All locally cached app data is cleared — this includes cached pages and stored app files, but not your transaction data.

3. The app checks the server for any updates and downloads the latest version if one is available.

4. The page reloads automatically. The app opens fresh, fully up to date, and re-downloads what it needs.

5. If anything goes wrong during the refresh, an alert appears asking you to reload the page manually.

---

## When to use it

Use this if:
- The app looks outdated or a new feature is not showing up
- Something in the app is not responding correctly
- You were asked to refresh after an update

---

## Note

Your transaction history and settings are not affected by a refresh. Only the app's local cache is cleared.
