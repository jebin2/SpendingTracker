# Manual Transaction

Lets you type in an expense by hand using a numeric keypad and simple form fields.

---

## How to use it

Tap the **+** button anywhere in the app to open the Add screen.

---

## What happens step by step

1. The Add screen opens instantly with a large number pad. Tap the digits to enter the amount — the total updates live as you type.

2. Scroll down to fill in the details: item name, quantity, merchant, category, payment method, date, time, and optional notes. Your most recently used category and payment method are pre-selected.

3. The merchant field shows chips for places you have visited recently — tap one to fill in the merchant and category at the same time.

4. Tap the **Save** button (or the tick icon). The transaction is saved and you are taken back to the dashboard.

5. If you are offline, the transaction is saved to your device and synced automatically the next time you have a connection. A small badge on the screen shows how many items are waiting to sync.

---

## Recurring transactions

When filling in the form, you can set a **Recurrence** (daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly). The dashboard will remind you to log the next occurrence when it comes due.

---

## Related features

Saved transactions appear immediately in the Transactions list, even before they sync to the cloud.
