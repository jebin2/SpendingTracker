# Google Sheet Settings

Found in **Settings → Google Sheet**. Shows the status of your connected Google Sheet and lets you manually sync.

---

## What you see

**Sheet link** — a direct link to open your Google Sheet in a browser or the Sheets app. All your transactions, categories, and analysis cache live in this sheet.

**Last synced time** — when the app last pulled fresh data from the sheet.

**Sync now button** — pulls the latest data from the sheet immediately. Useful if you made edits directly in the sheet and want to see them reflected in the app without waiting for the next automatic sync.

---

## How the sheet connection works

When you first sign in with Google, a new spreadsheet called **FundsFlee** is automatically created in your Google Drive. This sheet is yours — you own it, it lives in your account, and FundsFlee only has access to that one file.

Every transaction, category, setting, and analysis result is stored as rows in that sheet. The app reads from and writes to it directly using your Google account credentials.

---

## If something seems out of sync

Tap **Sync now** to force a fresh pull. If you are offline, sync will happen automatically the next time connectivity returns.
